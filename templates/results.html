Understanding the Results

Congestion Factor: A value of 2.0x means travel time is 2 times longer than normal. Higher values indicate worse traffic.
Percentage Change: Shows how much congestion increased or decreased compared to normal conditions.
Travel Time Impact: Indicates how much longer your journey will take on affected roads.
Severity Levels: Critical (>50% change), High (25-50%), Moderate (10-25%), Low (<10%).
